Rails Girls PoznańPrivacy Policy


We, the organizers of the Rails Girls Poznań workshops, need to collect and store data information submitted as a part of your application (based on Article 6 (1)(a) GDPR) in order to conduct the workshop.

This means that the candidates will be chosen based on their submitted applications, and participation confirmation messages will be sent to the email addresses specified in the applications. Also, in order to provide attendees with everything they need during the workshops we need to process other suitable data gathered via application form.

We collect the following information provided by you: your first and last name, email, telephone number, food preferences and allergies, programming level, operating system.

All personal data is used internally and won’t be shared with others. For further information on your rights, to withdraw consent, or to ask for deletion of your data, you can contact us. All your data will be removed from our database within two weeks after the event. Your data won’t be sent to any third party.
